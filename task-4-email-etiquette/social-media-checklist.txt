Social Media Checklist

 Do’s
Think before you post—consider how it may affect your reputation.
Keep your profiles professional, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions.
Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.
 Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Don’t engage in online harassment, trolling, or hate speech.
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
Don’t believe or forward everything without fact-checking.
Don’t spend excessive time scrolling—manage your screen time wisely.
