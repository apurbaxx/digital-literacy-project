# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and avoid reusing them.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, banking, and social media.
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unexpected payment requests (UPI safety).
- Always verify the receiver’s details before sending money through UPI apps (UPI safety).
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media.
- Download apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or App Store.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates and antivirus protection.
- Be careful while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for any unusual activity.
